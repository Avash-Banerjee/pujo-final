What We Do

We bring people together during the most magical time of the year — Durga Puja. Whether you're pandal-hopping, enjoying bhog, or dancing to the dhaak beats, Sharodiya-Swipe helps you find someone special to share the moments with.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I join this app?

Because let's be real, Pujo is not just about pandal-hopping. It's about having someone to share it with. Tired of seeing couples in matching outfits while you third-wheel? This app gives you a shot at changing that.

Why only for Pujo?

Because loneliness hits hardest during Pujo nights. Everyone's out, vibing, clicking couple selfies, and you're standing there holding your kurta pocket. This app flips that story for you.

What's the risk if I don't join the waitlist?

Nothing. Except another year of "Ebaro single?" jokes from your friends and aunties asking "biye kobe?" while you smile awkwardly.

How do matches work?

Swipe → Match → Chat. Same familiar flow, but with Pujo-themed vibes, filters, and location-based discovery so you can connect with people nearby while pandal-hopping.

Is it free to use?

Yes, completely free! We believe love (or at least Pujo companionship) shouldn't come with a price tag.
